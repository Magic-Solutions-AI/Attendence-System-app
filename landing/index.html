Getting Started

Up and Running in Minutes

No technical knowledge required. Just install, register, and start taking attendance.

1

Download & Install

Click the download button and run the installer. AttendX sets up everything automatically — Java, Python, and the face recognition engine.

2

Register Your Centre

Create an account for your coaching centre. Add tutor accounts so your team can log in and manage their batches independently.

3

Add Students

Add students one by one or bulk upload via Excel. Register each student's face using your laptop's webcam for face recognition.

4

Take Attendance

Open the Attendance page, switch on face scan mode, and let the AI automatically identify and mark present students in real time.
